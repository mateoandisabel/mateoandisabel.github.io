About

Mateo & Isabel is a collaboration between Elizabeth and Matt Lucas based in Charlotte, North Carolina. Formed in 2008 in Oakland, California, the idea behind Mateo & Isabel is fairly simple: design and make art about things we like and sell them for affordable prices so all can enjoy.

For wholesale orders, please contact Elizabeth at mateoandisabel@gmail.com. Wholesale orders can also be made through Etsy Wholesale.
